Our Service

OnTray is more than just your average delivery app.


On-Demand Food

Feeling hungry right now? OnTray will satisfy your cravings, matching you with a personal deliverer.

Real-Time Tracking

We monitor the location of our deliverers, providing updates on order statuses and restaurant delays.

Automated Payments

No need to worry about cash. OnTray automates the payment process, so you can focus on your meal.

Community Trust

Both buyers and deliverers can record their delivery experiences through our in-app rating system.

How does it work?


1. Place an order

Choose specific food or drink items from a restaurant on campus and indicate a delivery location.

2. Connect with a deliverer

OnTray matches you to a deliverer on campus. Your deliverer provides status updates throughout the process.

3. Receive your food

Your deliverer meets you where requested. Upon receiving the order, the payment is processed automatically.
